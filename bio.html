Joshua Weinberg

Joshua Weinberg is an expressive and dynamic flutist, composer, and collaborator based in New York City. He is Artistic Director of InfraSound, where he curates diverse and accessible programming for all of InfraSound’s concerts and outreach performances, rehearses the ensemble, connects with potential performance venues and collaborators, and works closely with the Executive Director promoting and acting on InfraSound’s mission. Joshua previously held the Artistic Director position with RenegadeEnsemble, a Minneapolis-based new music ensemble.

Joshua has performed nationally and internationally, including the European premiere of Sato Matsui’s flute sonata in Paris, a concert tour of world premieres through Germany with Apply Triangle, and concert performances in the U.S., Canada, Spain, Italy, and France. As a composer, his works have been described by colleagues as “lyrical, poetic, dramatic, and expressive.” His music weaves narratives with sound and he uses poetry and literature to influence and structure his compositions. Joshua’s works have been premiered in the U.S. and Canada, by e(L)ement duo, InfraSound, soprano Shannyn Rinker, RenegadeEnsemble, the dream songs project, vocalist Michael Betz, and flutist Catherine Ramirez.
